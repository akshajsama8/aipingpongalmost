AI PING PONG











Site Developed By: Akshaj || Game Developed By: Prashant Shukla

Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move your right wrist up and down, a red dot will appear on your wrist
  3. Press the play button, and move yourself 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  4. The red paddle will move based on how you move your wrist
